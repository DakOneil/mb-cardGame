A Card Game Where the Player With the Most Cards Wins!

How to play:

  • Click the 'New Game' button to start a deck.
  • Down below, click the 'Draw' button to play each round.
  • A player wins the round if their card is a higher value than their opponent's (Aces are high).
  • If the same value card is drawn by both players, the result is decided by the cards' suits, with the suits are valued high to low in this order:
    1. Spades
    2. Hearts
    3. Clubs
    4. Diamonds
  • After each round, the player that won keeps both cards drawn.
  • When the deck is empty, the player with the most cards won (loot) has won the game!
